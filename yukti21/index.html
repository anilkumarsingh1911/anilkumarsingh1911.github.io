Yukti

Yukti is the company engagement program started by the Office of Career Services (OCS), IIT Delhi. The events under Yukti aim to train and connect students to the professional world. Firms can hire IIT Delhi students under Yukti by conducting activities and extending offers to the fitting candidates.
